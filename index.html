About Taylored Instruction

At Taylored Instruction, we believe that proper training can save lives. That's why we offer comprehensive training programs in CPR, Lifeguarding, and Swimming—all tailored to meet the needs of our clients. Our certified instructors are dedicated to providing hands-on experience with a focus on real-life application, ensuring that every participant leaves ready to act in times of need.

As an employer, we value our team and are always looking for passionate individuals to join us in our mission to enhance health and safety preparedness. With us, you'll find a career that offers not just a job, but a chance to make a meaningful impact.

Our Training Programs

CPR & First-Aid Certification

Our CPR and First-Aid courses are designed for individuals seeking life-saving skills and knowledge. Our comprehensive programs are perfect for healthcare professionals, educators, and the general public.

Lifeguard Certification

Prepare for a rewarding career as a lifeguard with our certification program. You'll learn everything from pool safety to resuscitation, equipping you to safeguard lives in an aquatic environment.

Swimming Lessons

Dive into our swimming courses suitable for all ages and skill levels. Our expert instructors are committed to providing a supportive and educational environment for all swimmers.
