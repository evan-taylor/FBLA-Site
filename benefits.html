Frequently Asked Questions

Taylored Instruction provides a robust health benefits package aimed at enhancing the physical and mental well-being of our employees. Our medical coverage includes a wide range of preventative care services, emergency services, and specialty treatments, ensuring comprehensive care. Dental benefits cover routine check-ups, orthodontics, and emergency dental services, while our vision plan offers regular eye exams, prescription glasses, and contacts. These benefits are designed to support not only the health of our employees but also their families, which furthers our commitment to their overall well-being!

We encourage continuous learning through access to certification programs, professional courses, and internal training workshops. We always want you to be the best version of you, and we will help you get there however we can!

Taylored Instruction understands the importance of work-life balance and offers flexible working hours and remote work when possible. Due to the nature of our work, many classes must be taught in-person, but we will always allow you to do any prep work from home if you would like!

Life at Taylored Instruction

Joining Taylored Instruction means more than just a job. It’s an opportunity to be part of a family that cares deeply about making a difference. It’s a chance to be on the front lines of health and safety training, to help save lives and to grow with a company that values your well-being.

Our team is passionate, experienced, and driven by a shared mission to empower communities through education. We celebrate diversity, encourage creativity, and strive for excellence in everything we do.
